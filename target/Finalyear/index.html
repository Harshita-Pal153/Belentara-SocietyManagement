ICONIC LIFE IS HERE

A home is all about one's soul, self, and being. Belantara translates to the inner beauty of the forest, where we welcome you to experience a life blessed with inner beauty. The integrated township provides avant-garde homes equipped with lifestyle amenities to make sure your home is a complete world in itself.
